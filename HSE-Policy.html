Health,Safety & Environment Policy

Our Vision

A3M Contracts India Pvt. Ltd. intends to be acknowledged as a HSE in building and construction industry providing innovative solution to meet our customers and other interested parties satisfaction through our HSE performances and reliable services at the competitive cost, and enhances its reputation through continual growth and improvement in the Integrated Management System.

Our Principal

We conduct our businesses with respect and care for the environment and without compromising the health and safety of the people, whether employees, customers, and specially the public. We continuously improve our business process while meeting or exceeding legal or regulatory requirements and appropriate standards. We provide high quality products and services to make our customers successful and add value to our business. We provide an environment for open and transparent communication of HSE matters. We recognize that the skills and involvement of our employees are essential for understanding and fulfilling the needs of our company and customers

Our Commitment:

  • 1. Manage HSE effectively by developing, implementing and maintaining a best practice, process oriented, integrated management system.
  • 2. Assess and manage the risk of the business throughout product life cycle and the environmental impact from past practices
  • 3. Measure HSE performance and develop annual and long term HSE objectives to achieve continuous, sustainable improvement.
  • 4. Verify compliance with internal and external requirements through audits and strive for compliance by standards
  • 5. Address HSE issues and their impact on practices, processes and quality to align our business with client expectations.
  • 6. Promote HSE Awareness and enhance confidence of internal and external vendor partners in our business by informing, consulting, training & advising
  • 7. Hold every employee accountable for their commitment to our principles.
