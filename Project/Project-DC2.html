Nestled amidst the vibrant landscape of Whitefield, DC2 would span across three floors, encompassing a massive area of 60,000 square feet. Project Tips

  • Meticulous Planning: The success of DC2 relied on meticulous planning, where every detail, from server room layout to cooling systems, was carefully designed for optimal performance and efficiency.
  • Innovative Cooling Solutions: As the construction progressed, the team gained insights into the critical importance of advanced cooling solutions. Incorporating techniques like hot aisle containment and efficient ventilation systems, they achieved optimal energy consumption and environmental sustainability.
